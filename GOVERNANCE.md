# DGK Certification Specification – Governance

## Versioning
This specification follows **semantic law versioning**:

- MAJOR: Admissibility logic changes
- MINOR: Clarifications or extensions
- PATCH: Editorial or formatting fixes

## Change Authority
Changes MAY ONLY occur via:
- Explicit version bump
- Formal rationale
- Backward admissibility analysis

## Immutability Clause
No certified artifact may be retroactively invalidated
except by MAJOR version change.
