# Definitions

**Admissible**  
A system or decision satisfying ALL DGK invariants.

**Non-Admissible**  
Failure of ANY invariant.

**Invariant**  
A property that MUST hold under all conditions,
including stress, ambiguity, and adversarial review.

**Trace**  
A complete, replayable record of causal transformation.

**Paradox Preservation**  
The prohibition against forced collapse of mutually valid interpretations.

**κ–τ–Σ State**  
Ethical (κ), Temporal (τ), and Systemic Risk (Σ) coherence vector.
